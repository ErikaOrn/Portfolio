The Horder app was made as a mockup for a buy-and-sell platform that would prevent scalpers and scammers from taking advantage of collectors like myself.
in recent years more and more people have been taking up colelcting as a hobby, but as a result more and more bad actors have come on the scene to take advantage of collectors both new and seasoned.
Horder is my dream platform which is meant to bring power back to the buyer and away from scalpers and scammers, bringing order to your hoarding.
