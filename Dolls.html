Vanya - 16" Bisque Doll

This doll was a result of my free periods in my senior year schedule in high school.

That year I had finally taken ceramics and wanted to try my hand at making a ball jointed doll. I started with the ball joints and made the holes with straws, I then wrapped each joint with saran wrap once bone dry to help me mold the limbs around the joints.

Making her took about 3 months.

Honey - 2022

Of all of my customs she was the first that had a level of craftsmanship that I could be proud of.

Her hair is made of acrylic yarn wefted onto a wig, and beads were threaded on some of the hair as well.


Midnight - 2022

This doll was a result of me wanting a doll with some sick goth makeup, as I had been knee deep in my own goth "phase" (part of me will always be goth)

On a more self-indulgent note, i love the accent font i used for the title and subheadings.

Daria - 2023

This is among my most recent and (not coincidentally) most polished dolls

I found the base doll at goodwill and I immediately knew what my vision was: a pink girly girl with a heart motif.

Amaya - 2022

This doll came to me ina banged up state, with her upper eyelids somehow getting stained.

to remedy this i decided to use apoxie sculpt as the medium to cover up the stain, and as a means to reshape the eyes into a more dramatic shape.

Her super dramatic makeup draws me to her like a magnet, although she doesn't pass as well as a playline doll, I love her nonetheless.

Portrait Doll - 2023

One of my... odder dolls, she was made from a beat up draculaura i scored at my local GoodWill and her hair is... my own.

for context, for the longest time i never cut my hair, from when i was 7 until i was 17. i cut about 2 feet off and kept it because i knew i would use it for something, i just wasn't sure what.

however, one day i realized that i should make a mini-me doll, and knew immediately that i had my use for the spare hair. Do i recommend doing this? not unless you really want to and have a vaccum, because unlike regular doll hair, human hair gets everywhere and is hard to root into the head neatly.

Frankie - 2024

This frankie is my most recent custom to date, and perhaps much more understated than some of my others.

I wanted to make a more fashion oriented take on the newer Monster High Doll faces, and basically "yassified" Frankie.

Meowlody - 2022

This doll was a long time coming, i had made sketches for her about 6 months before i had the courage to start, and the first meowlody i used turned out to have a very weak head, and it split open and was sadly, irreparable.

off to eBay I went and after spenduing (what i consider) way too much money on one doll, I was able to get my meowlody base along with a hand combed wig made of acrylic yarn.

I was going for a more emo/scene look with a bit of more modern decora sensibilities sprinkled in, which in tthe end yielded this girlie! <3

Jeannie - 2024

And now my first American Girl Doll to have a custom face up.
I had already had hoards of American Girl Dolls by the time i got started on her, the difference with her however is that she was a failed custom from a friend to gacve her to me for free to do what I wanted with. And thus, came miss Jeannie!
