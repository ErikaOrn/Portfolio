Character Work:


Blaire & Dorothy


Blaire & Dorothy are two characters who have grown and evolved alongside me. Their initial concepts have been lost to time, however, nowadays they act as avatars for my love of fashion history. They have some sort of *gestures vaguely* time-immunity that gives me an excuse to imagine how these two would go about existing across the late 19th, 20th, and 21st centuries
