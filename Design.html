Wage the War on Poverty

This design was a project for my intro to communication design class, we were given a selection of stock images to choose from and the general parameters that the text must be black on white, and have two columns.

Additionally, we were required to make a cover page and I had to find a way to satisfy both the need for a cover image and legibility of the text.
When I first began this project I dreaded the task of having to balance content and graphics, but I think this assignment helped me learn the balance between the two.

On a more self-indulgent note, i love the accent font i used for the title and subheadings.

Krema Satellite Exhibiton

In the spring semester of my Junior year Lewis University was able to have their first satellite art exhibition at a local coffeehouse named Krema, although I had no art to share at the time, I wanted to contribute as the owner of Krema was my childhood neighbor, and I wanted to give back to both her, Krema, and to Lewis. This version of the poster is the one that was sent in promotional posters across the university.

CoHFAC Kickback

This is an image i made for the Lewis University College of Humanities, Fine Arts, and Communications Instagram story to promote our second annual fundraiser: CoHFAC Kickback! I used a mix of new assets made by both myself and my peers along with assets that were carried over from the previous year's branding.

CoHFAC Kickback

This is an image i made to be printed out for the aforementioned CoHFAC Kickback fundraiser event. This poster was promoting an activity we had with our creative writing students to be able to make a poem on the fly with a prompt or even just a word! I used the Instagram story image as a base for this one, however it should be noted that the bottom third of the image seen here was cropped out of the final poster.

One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest Project

This was an early project for my typography class from my Sophomore year. We were asked to choose a book and design a brand new cover, thus, I chose One Flew Over the Cuckoo's net as it was my favorite book I read in High School and decided it needed it's flowers.

Rage Against

This project was born out of a prompt to create something using Swiss Design sensibilities, and seeing as my professor at the time was obsessed with AI, my stubborn self decided to make this whole project revolve around my refusal to use AI in any of my schoolwork.

Jack.

This project was meant to hone our skills in making a type based logo, so i used a fake brand called Jack., which I made way back as a kid in a skateboarding game to be... well a skateboarding brand.

Romeoville Night Market

This poster is for a fictional night market for the very real town that I live in.
I wanted the vibes to be cozy, but still fresh and breathable, and I think the way I made the text bend and contour helps get that across.
