Erika Ornelas

I'm a multidisciplinary artist who specializes in traditional and digital illustration, doll design, and general graphic design.

Additionally, I have been trained in publication design, UI/UX design, typography, painting, and traditional sculpture. My liberal arts education at Lewis University has helped further diversify my skillsets outside of the design realm.

I aim make designs that not only look good, but feel good. I aim to serve clients with designs that can elevate their product and/or business to a higher level of aesthetic appeal, while remaining true to my ethos as a designer.
