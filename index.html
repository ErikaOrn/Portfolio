Erika: herself & her work


Hi! I'm Erika, I'm a multimedia artist and designer with an affinity for the eclectic. For as long as I have been able to create, I have loved to do so; all of my work contains bits and pieces of myself, informed by my own life experience. My work isn't bound by any category, as I take the multimedia label to heart; my creations span from illustration, to recycled one-of-a-kind dolls, to hand-coded websites, such as the one you're visiting right now! My overarching goal as an artist and designer is to bridge the gap between goods and art, to allow as many everyday people as possible to enjoy, experience, and own fine art.
